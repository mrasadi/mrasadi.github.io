Research Experience & Academic Projects
======

* **Multi-Depot Vehicle Routing Problem (MDVRP)**

  Contributed to a C++ benchmark suite covering 240 routing instances. Implemented ALNS and GRASP methods, achieving near-optimal or optimal solutions across a wide range of scenarios.

* **Orienteering Problem with Hotel Selection**  
  Implemented and evaluated multiple metaheuristic approaches (GA, ACO, GRASP, SA, Tabu Search) to balance cost, distance, and user preferences.
